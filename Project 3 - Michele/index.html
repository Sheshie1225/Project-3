"IT'S SHARK WEEK!"


The White Shark, more commonly referred to as the “Great White” has been reported to be involved in more attacks on humans than any other shark. Great white sharks are the most aggressive sharks in the world having recorded 333 attacks on humans, with 52 of them being fatal. Although you’re more likely to get killed by lightning or even cows who kill about 20 people yearly, the fear of death by a shark attack is universal.
